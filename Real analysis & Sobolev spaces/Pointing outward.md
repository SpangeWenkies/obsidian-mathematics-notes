
# Definition: Pointing outward

**Type:** #definition  

## Definition
We say that $\nu(x)$ **points outward** if it forms a positive (acute) angle with all vectors pointing from Ω to the exterior

Equivalently:

- $\nu(x)$ is [[Orthogonality|orthogonal]] to the [[Tangent hyperplane]] at $x$, and
- it points into the complement $\mathbb{R}^{n}\setminus \Omega$, not into $\Omega$
