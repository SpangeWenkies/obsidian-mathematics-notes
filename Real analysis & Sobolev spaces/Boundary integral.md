
# Definition: Boundary integral

**Type:** #definition  

## Definition
- The [[Boundary]] integral is given as: $$\int_{\partial \Omega}\dots dS$$
- This means we integrate over the [[Surface]] enclosing the [[Openness|open]] set $\Omega$ 
- $\partial \Omega$ is the boundary of the [[Domain]] $\Omega$
