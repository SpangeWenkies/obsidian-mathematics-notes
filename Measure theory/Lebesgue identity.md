
# Definition: Lebesgue identity

**Type:** #definition  

## Definition
- $\int^{\infty}_{0}m(A_{t})dt=\int f(x)dm(x)$, 
	where $A_{t}$ are horizontal slices and $m$ is a [[Lebesgue measure]] 

## Intuition (optional)
- the vertical integration area is actually equal to the "sum" over the horizontal slices
