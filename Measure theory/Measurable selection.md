
# Definition: Measurable selection

**Type:** #definition  

## Definition
- see [[Measure]]
- The measurable selection theorem gives a function mapping $x \mapsto K(x,.)$, s.t.,
	- $\forall x, K(x,.)\in$ [[Set-valued map]] $\Phi(x)$ 
	- $\forall$ [[Borel set]] $B$, the mapping $x \mapsto K(x,B)$ is [[Measurability|measurable]]
