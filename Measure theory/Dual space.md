
# Definition: Dual space

**Type:** #definition  

## Definition
- Given a [[Normed vector space]] $X$ , the dual (space) $X^{*}$ is the space of all continuous linear functions on $X$ , i.e., $X^{*}$ is all functions $f:X\mapsto \mathbb{R}$ or $(\mathbb{C})$ continuously

## Intuition (optional)
- We get that $X=\mathbb{R}^{n} \implies X^{*}=\mathbb{R}^{n}$ as the continuous linear transformation stays in $\mathbb{R}^n$
- We get that $X=L^{p}\implies X^{*}=L^{q}\text{ where } \frac{1}{p}+\frac{1}{q}=1$
